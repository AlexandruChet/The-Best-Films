"Avengers: Endgame": Recognition at the awards

"Avengers: Endgame" received Academy Award nominations in the categories for Best Visual Effects. The film also won several awards at the People's Choice Awards, including Best Picture, and won the Best Cast Award from the Screen Actors Guild (SAG Awards). In addition, the film was recognized at the Critics' Choice Awards and MTV Movie & TV Awards for Best Fight Scene, Best Superhero Film and other achievements.
