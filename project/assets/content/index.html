Is it real? "Titanic" is a British passenger steamship. At the time of its construction, it was the largest liner in the world. It sank on its maiden voyage from Southampton to New York after hitting an iceberg on the night of 14/15 April 1912. More than 1,500 people died as a result of the disaster.
