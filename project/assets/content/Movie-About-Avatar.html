Your Name Please

  • Jake Sally, replacing his brother in the "Avatar" program, gets the opportunity to control the body of a hybrid of a human and Na'va, created for the study of Pandora and interaction with local residents. At first, Jake works for the RDA corporation and the military, but as he gets to know the Na'va culture and way of life, his views begin to change.
  • During his missions in the body of the avatar, Jake meets Neytira, who saves his life and brings him to his tribe. Gradually, he learns the language, culture and traditions of the Na'iva, winning their trust and respect. Jake falls in love with Neytiri and begins to realize that the corporation's plans to mine unobtanium will cause irreparable damage to Pandora and its inhabitants.
  • When the corporation's plans become clear, and preparations begin for the destruction of the sacred tree, under which huge deposits of unobtanium are located, Jake decides to side with the Na'va. Together with Grace and other scientists, he tries to convince the leadership of the RDA to abandon aggressive expansion, but fails.
  • A large-scale battle begins between the Na'vi and RDA forces. Despite the technological superiority of humans, the Na'vi, united under the leadership of Jake, use their deep connection with the nature of Pandora to fight the invaders. As a result, thanks to the intervention of the natural forces of Pandora and the persistence of the Na'va, people suffer defeat and are forced to leave the planet.
